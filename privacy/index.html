Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

You Go Boom is a single-player puzzle game developed by Seth Weitberg. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera. It does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The app stores a small amount of gameplay data locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults system, including:

  • Whether you have completed the first-run tutorial
  • Your daily-puzzle history, current streak, and best streak
  • Sound and gameplay preferences
  • Your progress through beginner and challenge modes

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to us or to any third party. You can erase it at any time by deleting the app.

3. Network Activity

The app does not make any network requests. It works fully offline.

4. Children's Privacy

You Go Boom is suitable for general audiences. Because the app does not collect, transmit, or store personal information of any kind, it does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

5. Third Parties

We do not share information with third parties because we do not collect any.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
